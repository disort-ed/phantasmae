The Exhibition

An Ancient Mystery
Reimagined

Phantasma is a world-class exhibition dedicated to the art, history, and folklore of spectral phenomena. Through rare artefacts, immersive installations, and historical accounts, we invite you to explore humanity's oldest obsession.

From Victorian séance parlours to documented hauntings, from Japanese yūrei to Celtic banshees — every culture has its ghosts. Here, they come alive.

Operating since 1987, our collection spans over 2,000 artefacts and curated experiences across twelve distinct chambers.

The Whitmore
Haunting

In 1893, the Whitmore family reported a series of inexplicable events in their Derbyshire manor. This special exhibition presents original photographs, correspondence, and objects recovered from the estate — never before shown to the public.

Featuring first-person accounts, investigator journals, and an audio reconstruction of the infamous November séance.
